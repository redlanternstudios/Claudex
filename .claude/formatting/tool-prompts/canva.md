## GRID & SAFE ZONES
- Grid: 12 columns, 80px margin, 24px gutter
- Safe zone: keep ALL critical content 80px inset from all canvas edges
- For social: double safe zone to 120px — crop risk on platform previews
- Bleed elements (backgrounds, full-width images) can extend to canvas edge

## TYPOGRAPHY
| Role        | Size    | Weight  |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| Display     | 64–72px | 900     |
| H1          | 48px    | 700–800 |
| H2          | 36px    | 700     |
| H3          | 28px    | 600     |
| Body        | 16–18px | 400     |
| Caption     | 12–14px | 400     |

- Font: Inter or Geist
- Heading tracking: -0.02em (tight)
- Minimum legible size for print/export: 12px

- Cream (light):  #F7F2EE
- Cream dark:   #07080D (text on cream)

## LAYOUT RULES
- One primary visual hierarchy per canvas — one H1, one dominant focal point
- Accent red: use once per canvas, max twice
- White space is intentional — do not fill every corner
- Dark backgrounds default for app/product contexts
- Cream/light backgrounds for documents, proposals, editorial

## EXPORT SETTINGS
- Screen / digital: PNG at 2x (Export at 2x for retina)
- Print: PDF Print with bleed
- Presentation: Download as PDF or PPTX

## ANTI-PATTERNS
- No clipart or stock illustration in RLS materials
- No rainbow or gradient text
- No more than 2 font families per canvas
- No text in safe zone violation
- No gradient backgrounds unless approved dark-to-transparent cinema style
