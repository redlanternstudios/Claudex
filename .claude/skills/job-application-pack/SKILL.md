# JOB APPLICATION PACK

**Triggers:** `job application pack`, `jobpack`, `/jobpack`, `tailorjob`, `/tailorjob`, `tailor resume`, or a live job URL paired with resume, cover letter, candidate profile, or application package intent.

**Command:** `.claude/commands/jobpack.md`
**Status SSOT:** `OPS/status/HireWire_Career_Command_Center.md`

## Purpose

Turn one verified job URL into Rory Semeah's complete company tailored application package. Never confuse the standalone one page resume with the combined three page packet.

## Required package

1. One page cover letter.
2. One page ATS resume.
3. One page company candidate profile.
4. One combined three page application packet.
5. Editable DOCX sources for the three component documents.
6. Fit summary, missing evidence warnings, application questions, and upload guidance.

## Fixed packet order

Cover Letter, then Resume, then Company Candidate Profile.

The standalone resume remains the primary ATS upload. The combined packet is the human facing narrative: intent, evidence, then differentiated company fit.

## Candidate profile title

Use exactly:

`Rory Semeah | <Company> Candidate Profile`

Filename:

`Rory_Semeah_<Company>_Candidate_Profile.pdf`

## Execution

1. Verify the live posting and record the exact company, title, job ID, team, location, compensation, requirements, stack, application questions, and status.
2. Research only official company sources needed for product, culture, terminology, role context, and restrained brand tokens.
3. Build a private requirement to evidence matrix. Mark each requirement direct, adjacent, or missing.
4. Use only verified Rory evidence. Preserve employment titles, dates, metrics, education, and certification status.
5. Apply subtle company brand influence. Never create an official looking company document.
6. Build all component files, then combine them in the fixed order.
7. Render every page. Inspect full page and mobile scale. Correct touching text, crowding, clipping, tiny type, weak gutters, and poor page balance.
8. Extract PDF text and verify ATS reading order, page counts, filenames, and combined order.
9. Deliver the whole package in one shot. Never stop with an offer to make a cleaner version.

## Resume contract

Preserve the approved one page visual structure: identity, target headline, contacts, selected builds, summary, technology grid, linear ATS skills, professional experience, education and certifications.

Minimum targets:

* Body and contact text at least 8 pt.
* Role headings at least 9 pt.
* Section headings at least 10 pt.
* At least 11 points between the technology grid and the first capability line.
* At least 5 points between the capability block and Professional Experience.

Never solve overflow by silently shrinking below 7.8 pt. Icons may supplement skill text but never replace selectable ATS text.

## Candidate profile contract

Use this sequence:

1. Candidate snapshot.
2. What I bring.
3. Why the company.
4. Five item proof map.
5. Relevant selected work or career snapshot.

The profile answers why Rory for this company and role. It must not duplicate the resume.

## Truth gate

Do not claim direct Vercel AI SDK, Vercel Workflows, Turborepo, advanced caching optimization, serverless function debugging, open source contributions, developer advocacy, sales ownership, or other unverified work without Rory's confirmation.

Do not turn familiarity into implementation, team exposure into personal ownership, planned work into completed work, or product responsibility into unsupported engineering claims.

## Failure conditions

Reject the output if any component exceeds one page, the combined packet is not exactly three pages, the order is wrong, branding is heavy handed, claims are unsupported, text touches another element, ATS extraction fails, editable sources are absent, or the response ends with an offer instead of the completed package.
