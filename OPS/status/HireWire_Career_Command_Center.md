# HireWire Career Command Center

Date: 2026-07-19
Status: ACTIVE
Owner: Rory Lee Semeah

## Purpose

Turn every verified job URL into a complete, company tailored application package without losing the master resume format, career evidence, or settled delivery decisions.

## Canonical trigger

`/jobpack <job URL>`

Aliases: `/tailorjob <job URL>`, `tailor resume <job URL>`.

## Locked package

Every job URL produces:

1. One page cover letter PDF and editable DOCX.
2. One page ATS resume PDF and editable DOCX.
3. One page company candidate profile PDF and editable DOCX.
4. One combined three page application pack PDF.
5. Fit summary, missing evidence warnings, application questions, and upload guidance.

## Combined order

Cover Letter, then Resume, then Company Candidate Profile.

The standalone resume remains the primary ATS upload. The combined packet is for a portfolio field, additional documents field, direct recruiter outreach, or email.

## Candidate profile naming

Displayed title:

`Rory Semeah | <Company> Candidate Profile`

Filename:

`Rory_Semeah_<Company>_Candidate_Profile.pdf`

## Brand decision

Use subtle company influence only. Adapt accent color, rules, borders, restrained heading treatment, spacing character, and small geometric cues. Never imitate an official company document or imply endorsement.

## Resume rules

* One US Letter page.
* Preserve the approved master structure and technology grid.
* Rewrite headline, summary, skill priority, and bullets for the live role.
* Keep historical titles and dates unchanged.
* Repeat critical skill words as selectable linear ATS text.
* Keep body and contact text at least 8 pt, role headings at least 9 pt, and section headings at least 10 pt.
* Maintain visible gutters after the technology grid and before Professional Experience.
* Never invent technologies, metrics, certifications, customers, or outcomes.

## Candidate profile structure

1. Candidate snapshot.
2. What I bring.
3. Why the company.
4. Five item proof map.
5. Relevant selected work or career snapshot.

The profile is a human facing fit brief, not a duplicate resume.

## Quality gate

Before delivery:

* Render every page.
* Inspect full page and mobile scale.
* Confirm no text touches cards, rules, page edges, or adjacent sections.
* Extract PDF text and verify ATS reading order.
* Confirm each component is exactly one page.
* Confirm combined packet is exactly three pages in the locked order.
* Confirm editable sources exist.
* Reject any partial package or response that ends by offering to finish later.
