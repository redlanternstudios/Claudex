# Amina Growth System

Status: DRAFT

## What We Are Starting

We are starting one owned growth system for Amina.

It will help us get found, get clicked, and get installs across:

1. TikTok
2. Instagram
3. Facebook
4. App Store search
5. Website search
6. Email and retargeting

## Why We Are Starting It

Right now, growth is too manual.

This system is meant to:

1. Reduce dependence on outside marketers
2. Turn one idea into many channel specific outputs
3. Learn from real data faster
4. Keep the brand consistent
5. Save time and budget

## How It Works

Think of it as one source feeding many channels.

### Simple flow

1. Inputs come in
   1. Product updates
   2. Reviews
   3. Analytics
   4. Competitor activity
   5. Search trends
   6. App Store data

2. SwarmClaw studies the inputs
   1. Finds keywords
   2. Spots patterns
   3. Drafts captions
   4. Drafts scripts
   5. Suggests metadata
   6. Suggests page updates

3. Humans approve the public pieces
   1. Nothing goes out blindly
   2. Taste and safety stay human owned

4. The content goes to the right places
   1. TikTok
   2. Instagram
   3. Facebook
   4. App Store pages
   5. Website pages
   6. Email

5. Results come back in
   1. Views
   2. Clicks
   3. Installs
   4. Trials
   5. Retention
   6. Shares

6. The next round gets better
   1. We keep what works
   2. We cut what does not
   3. We repeat the loop

## What SwarmClaw Does

SwarmClaw should handle the repeatable work.

It should:

1. Research
2. Draft
3. Compare
4. Monitor
5. Report

It should not:

1. Post blindly
2. Make final brand decisions
3. Skip approval
4. Replace human judgment

## What This Gives Us

If this works, we get:

1. More visibility
2. More installs
3. Better content consistency
4. Faster learning
5. Lower operating cost
6. Less dependence on outside people

## Who Does What

| Area | Owner | Job |
|---|---|---|
| Strategy | Humans | Decide what matters |
| Research | SwarmClaw | Find signals and patterns |
| Drafting | SwarmClaw | Write channel specific content |
| Approval | Humans | Approve public output |
| Publishing | Automation | Send content to the right channel |
| Measurement | Automation | Track what happened |
| Learning | Humans plus SwarmClaw | Decide the next move |

## First Step

Start with one small loop:

1. Research
2. Draft
3. Approve

5. Measure
6. Improve

## Bottom Line

This is not just marketing software.

It is a repeatable growth system that helps Amina become easier to find, easier to trust, and easier to install.
